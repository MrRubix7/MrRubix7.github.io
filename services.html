Our Services

At Maharba Construction, we offer a wide range of construction services to meet your needs. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering high-quality results on time and within budget.

  • Residential Construction: We specialize in building custom homes, renovations, and additions that reflect your unique style and preferences.
  • Commercial Construction: From office buildings to retail spaces, we have the expertise to handle commercial projects of all sizes.
  • Project Management: Our project management services ensure that your construction project runs smoothly from start to finish, with clear communication and efficient coordination.
  • Green Building: We are committed to sustainable construction practices and can help you incorporate eco-friendly features into your project.

Our Specialties

In addition to our core construction services, we also offer specialized services to enhance your project:

  • Design-Build: We provide integrated design and construction services to streamline the building process and ensure a cohesive vision.
  • Renovation and Remodeling: Whether you're looking to update your kitchen, bathroom, or entire home, we can help transform your space.
  • Custom Carpentry: Our skilled carpenters can create custom cabinetry, trim work, and other wood features to add character to your project.
  • Concrete Services: We offer concrete pouring, finishing, and repair services for foundations, driveways, patios, and more.
  • Painting: Our painting professionals can transform your space with a fresh coat of paint, enhancing the look and feel of your home or business.
  • Drywall Installation: Our drywall specialists can handle all aspects of drywall installation, from initial framing to final finishing.
